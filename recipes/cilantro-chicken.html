Slow Cooker Lime Cilantro Chicken

Description

This slow cooker cilantro-lime chicken is bursting with flavor. Try it in tacos for a nice change from plain chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package dry taco seasoning mix
  • 1 medium lime juiced
  • 3 tablespoons of chopped cilantro
  • 3 pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients.

  2. Mix salsa, taco seasoning, lime juice and cilantro in a slow cooker until well combined.
    Add chicken and spoon salsa mixture over the top to coat.

  3. Cover and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the centre and juices run clear,
    on Low for 6-8 hours or High for 4 hours. An instant red thermometer inserted
    in the centre should read 165 degrees F (72 degrees C)

  4. Shred Chicken in the crock with two forks, then mix with sauce
  5. Enjoy!
