Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe is regarded as being the most popular on allrecipes; it's also highly rated,
so prepare for an explosion of flavours and enjoy this delicious comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (halal) italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomatoe sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomatoe paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of chopped parsley (divided)
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of dried basil leaves
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients.

  2. Mix salsa, taco seasoning, lime juice and cilantro in a slow cooker until well combined.
    Add chicken and spoon salsa mixture over the top to coat.

  3. Cover and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the centre and juices run clear,
    on Low for 6-8 hours or High for 4 hours. An instant red thermometer inserted
    ` in the centre should read 165 degrees F (72 degrees C)

  4. Shred Chicken in the crock with two forks, then mix with sauce
  5. Enjoy!
